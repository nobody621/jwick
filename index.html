Frequently Asked Questions

What does 'fully reconditioned' mean?

Our reconditioning involves a complete service: movement disassembly, cleaning, oiling, calibration, replacement of worn parts with authentic or high-quality equivalents, and case/bracelet restoration to preserve character.

Are all your watches authentic?

Yes. Authenticity is paramount. Every watch is meticulously verified by our experts before being offered for sale. We stand by our guarantee.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we offer secure international shipping. Shipping costs and times vary by destination. Please contact us for specific inquiries.

What is your warranty policy?

We offer a 12-month mechanical warranty on all our reconditioned timepieces covering the movement against defects under normal use conditions.
